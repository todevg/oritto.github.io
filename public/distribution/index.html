Application information

Prepare these details before contacting the team.

  • Enquiry type: depot, distributor, or dealer
  • Business entity and owner or promoter name
  • GST number and primary contact details
  • Country, state, city, town, complete address, and PIN code
  • Years of business experience
  • Annual business sales or turnover range
  • Proposed investment range
  • Current distribution network and relevant category experience

Do not send identity documents, bank details, passwords, or payment information in an initial enquiry. The ORITTO team should confirm any later document process through a verified channel.
