The ORITTO direction

A consumer lighting brand built for real applications.

ORITTO’s website brief calls for a vibrant, professional brand with a broad product base, practical support, and a clear connection between manufacturing and the spaces products serve.

Our journey

From product idea to complete lighting portfolio.

ORITTO brings consumer, professional, architectural, industrial, and solar categories into one portfolio. The brand direction is centred on helping customers and project teams move from application need to suitable product category and direct enquiry.

Manufacturing excellence through backward integration

The Mandideep manufacturing brief emphasizes closer control over processes, component choices, assembly, testing, and ongoing product development. Detailed claims, process diagrams, plant imagery, and certifications will be published only after stakeholder approval.
